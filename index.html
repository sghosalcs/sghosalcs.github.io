Shuvam Ghosal

Shuvam Ghosal is an Associate Application Developer at Automatic Data Processing, Inc. (ADP) . He received his MS in Computer Science from the University of Southern California in 2023.

Before joining USC, he obtained his bachelor’s degree in computer science and engineering from MAKAUT in 2021.

His work is at the intersection of machine learning, computer vision and natural language processing.
